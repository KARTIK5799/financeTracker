Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions. You will receive an email with a link to create a new password.

What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?

If you encounter a technical issue, please contact our support team at support@financetracker.com with a description of the issue, and we will assist you as soon as possible.

Can I update my account information?

Yes, you can update your account information by logging into your account and navigating to the "Account Settings" section. From there, you can make changes to your personal information.

Where can I find my transaction history?

Your transaction history can be found in the "Transaction History" section of your account dashboard. You can view and filter your transactions from there.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact customer support by sending an email to support@financetracker.com or by calling +1 234 567 8900. Our team is available to help you with any inquiries.
